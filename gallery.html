Tips Preview

Small things I have learned while practicing and growing.

Tip 1: Notice the light before you touch your settings. Soft, natural light changes everything.
Tip 2: Move your body before adjusting your camera. Sometimes a new angle makes all the difference.
Tip 3: Give yourself space to experiment. Not every photo needs to be perfect to be meaningful.

Table Section

A simple cheat sheet of what I try when I’m practicing.

When I'm shooting… I usually try… So the photo feels…
In low light Raising ISO a little Brighter (without using flash)
Something is moving A faster shutter speed Less blurry and more clear
Portraits A lower aperture (if possible) Softer in the background
