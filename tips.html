What I'm Learning

This page is a simple collection of tips that help me when I'm taking photos. I'm still learning, so these are not “perfect rules”..just small things that make photography feel less overwhelming and more fun to practice.

Quick Tips

  • Start with light: Soft natural light (like near a window) can make photos look instantly better.
  • Move first: Try stepping closer, backing up, or changing angles before changing camera settings.
  • Take more than one shot: I usually take a few photos of the same thing and compare what worked.

Practice Cheat Sheet (Table)

This table shows what I usually try depending on the situation.

When I’m shooting… I usually try… So the photo feels…
In low light Raising ISO a little Brighter (without using flash)
Something is moving A faster shutter speed Less blurry and more clear
Portraits A lower aperture (if possible) Softer in the background

I'm still practicing these, but this helps me remember what to try first.
